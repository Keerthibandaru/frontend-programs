Mechanical Engineering

At SVIET College, the Mechanical Engineering program is designed to provide students with the breadth and depth of knowledge and skills required to excel in the design, fabrication, and maintenance of a wide range of objects, from everyday items like safety pins to cutting-edge technologies like spacecraft. Our curriculum caters to those who have a passion for working with both massive machines and intricate precision instruments and devices.
